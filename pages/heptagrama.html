What meaning does the talisman have?

The talisman is an object that is intentionally charged with magical powers for specific purposes and used to attract specific energies. The talisman generally contains inscriptions or engravings that are intended to represent a symbolism in accordance with the intention that is to be impregnated.

The Heptagrama is the center of the talisman, it is the one that receives and sends energy.
The Heptagram as a supreme symbol connects realities in the universe through energy.
Along with it are the words that give meaning to our deepest desires.
Remember, there is no good or bad energy, it's just how that energy is channeled, be careful what you wish for.
